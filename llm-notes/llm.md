Here are some useful resources for LLM (Master of Laws) notes:

1. [KLE Society's Law College Study Materials](https://www.klelawcollege.org/study-materials/): This site offers a variety of study materials for different law subjects, including Administrative Law, Banking Law, Constitutional Law, and more.
2. [Delhi University LLM Course Materials](https://lawfaculty.du.ac.in/Students/LL.M.-Course-Materials): This page provides updated course materials for various LLM subjects such as Comparative Constitutional Law, International Organisation & Human Rights, Corporate Management and Social Responsibility, and more.
3. [Legal Bites Law Library](https://www.legalbites.in/law-library): This resource offers comprehensive study materials for LLB, LLM, Judiciary, and entrance exams. It includes notes on subjects like Corporate Law, International Law, Law of Torts, and more.

These resources should help you with your LLM studies. If you need notes on a specific topic, feel free to ask!
