Sure! Here are some resources that can help you with your NLP studies:

1. [Natural Language Processing Unit-I](https://mgit.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/Natural-Language-Processing-by-Dr-A-Nagesh.pdf): This document covers the structure of words, morphological models, sentence boundary detection, and more.
2. [Natural Language Processing: part 1 of lecture notes](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/teaching/2002/NatLangProc/nlp1-4.pdf): This set of lecture notes introduces fundamental NLP techniques, including finite-state techniques, part-of-speech tagging, parsing, and generation.
3. [Unit -1 NLP-R20 - Notes](https://www.studocu.com/in/document/jawaharlal-nehru-technological-university-anantapur/natural-language-processing/unit-1-nlp-r20-notes/114030580): This document provides an overview of natural language, its components, and various NLP techniques.

These resources should give you a solid foundation in NLP. If you have any specific topics or questions, feel free to ask!
